About Us

Founded in 2020, Future Fitness's mission is to make strength training accessible to everyone.
As part of this mission, we're determined to cut through the clutter of today's fad workout culture with our science-based strength training methodology.
Exercising doesn't have to be complicated or intimidating - that's why we're focused on keeping our method simple, straightforward, and approachable.
We work with amazing people like you, to create an environment which ensures that health and wellness are at the cultural centre of your organisation.
For employers, we provide tailored activities including workshops, webinars, engagement programmes and an online community with access to
tools and support. We take a 'one size fits one' approach, as every person's needs are different. After asking each participant to benchmark themselves,
we support them as they make small changes in each of The Four Elements - Eat, Sweat, Think, Connect - and as their energy levels and attitudes
transform, so they inspire and engage more of the people around them.
